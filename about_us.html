About us

At Chimera Chat, we help students connect and share. Our team, Flora, Louise Mona & Emelie work to make chatting and learning easier for everyone. With our chat, students can talk to each other, share ideas, and upload or download PDF files. It’s a simple way to study together and help each other. Join Chimera Chat and make learning easier!
