FAQ

What is ChimeraChat?

ChimeraChat is a web application where users can create accounts, log in, chat with each other, and share files.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, go to the Sign Up page and fill out the form with your details.

How do I log in to ChimeraChat?

To log in to ChimeraChat, go to the Log In page and enter your username and password.

What is the About Us page?

The About Us page provides information about ChimeraChat and the team behind it.

What can I do on the Chat page?

On the Chat page, you can send messages to other users, join chat rooms, and share files.

What can I do on the Files page?

On the Files page, you can upload files, and manage and view previously uploaded files
